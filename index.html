Statuette

A family tradition of picking the Academy Award winners.

Description

My family has a tradition every year to try and pick the Academy Award winners. I am in charge of the voting and have usually digitized it by using a form application on the web. This year, however, I wanted to code the entire experience myself.

I had just learned about Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) in an accelerated web development course that I was taking and wanted to solidify my knowledge of OOP. I started coding this with about a week before the Oscars and was able to get most of the base funcionality working.

I was able to send it out to family and friends and I was elated that they not only had fun with the app but came back multiple times. Since then, I have been adding their feature requests and improving the code to allow for more functionality and flexibilty next year.

Food Roulette

An app that simplifies the way you find restaurants near you.

Description

Have you ever tried to find dinner with a loved one and you find your self going to the same restaurants over and over again but want to find something new? That is where Food Roulette comes in.

Food Roulette aggregates the restaurants around you so that you can focus on finding the right food for any occasion. Simpley pick a category of food and we serve you one restaurant at a time to choose from.

The app uses locaiton data to find where you are and then passes those coordinates to the Yelp API to pull restaurants in your area. Once you select the restaurant you would like to know more about we allow you to see it on a map as well as Yelp reviews for that particular restaurant so that you can make an educated decision.
